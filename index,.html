Johannesburg, South Africa

Johannesburg, often referred to as Jo'burg or Jozi, is the largest city in South Africa and one of the 50 largest urban areas in the world. It is the provincial capital of Gauteng, which is the wealthiest province in South Africa. Johannesburg is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and economic significance. The city has a diverse population and is a major hub for commerce, finance, and industry in the country. It is also home to many cultural attractions, including museums, theaters, and art galleries.
